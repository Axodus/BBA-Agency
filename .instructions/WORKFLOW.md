# Axodus BBA Workflow

## Workflow Philosophy

BBA workflows must remain:

- structured
- auditable
- governance-aware
- strategically aligned
- institutionally responsible

---

# Communication Lifecycle

## 1. Request or Initiative

Communication may originate from:

- Governance
- Business
- Academy
- Marketplace
- ACS
- Treasury
- Partnerships
- Ecosystem campaigns

---

## 2. Strategic Review

The communication must be reviewed for:

- narrative alignment
- governance compatibility
- treasury sensitivity
- compliance concerns
- reputational impact

---

## 3. Content Production

Content may include:

- articles
- announcements
- reports
- educational materials
- campaign assets
- institutional communication
- media kits

---

## 4. Review Process

Review may involve:

- governance validation
- legal review
- treasury review
- brand review
- compliance validation

depending on sensitivity.

---

## 5. Distribution

Approved communication is distributed through:

- websites
- social channels
- newsletters
- ecosystem portals
- partner channels
- enterprise channels

---

## 6. Analytics

After publication:

- engagement is tracked
- reach is measured
- sentiment is monitored
- campaign performance is analyzed

---

## 7. Reporting

BBA should periodically publish:

- campaign reports
- ecosystem communication reports
- growth reports
- transparency reports

---

# ACS-Assisted Workflow

ACS agents may assist with:

- content drafts
- media analysis
- scheduling
- telemetry
- analytics
- reporting

ACS agents must not autonomously publish sensitive institutional communication without approval.
